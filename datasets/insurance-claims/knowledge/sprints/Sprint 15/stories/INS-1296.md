## Description

As a Claims Operations Manager, I need the Agentforce AI assistant to surface next-best-action recommendations for Claim__c records that have been in FNOL status beyond the configured SLA threshold so that my team can prioritize intervention, reduce cycle time, and maintain compliance with state-mandated acknowledgment timelines.

The AI recommendations should consider claim complexity indicators (number of Claim_Line__c records, presence of SIU flags, COB indicators, and provider credentialing status) to suggest the most appropriate action: escalate to SIU, request additional documentation, assign to senior adjuster, or auto-adjudicate.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Claim__c record where Claim__c.Status__c = 'FNOL' and Claim__c.FNOL_Received_Date__c is more than X hours ago (X configured via custom metadata Claim_SLA_Config__mdt.FNOL_Acknowledgment_Hours__c),
When the Agentforce Claims AI panel is rendered on the Claim__c record page,
Then a next-best-action card is displayed with a recommended action label, confidence score (%), and a brief AI-generated rationale (max 280 characters).

Given Claim__c.SIU_Flag__c = true,
When the AI evaluates the claim,
Then the recommended action must be 'Escalate to SIU' with confidence >= 85%, and the action card displays a warning badge.

Given Claim__c.COB_Indicator__c = true AND the rendering provider's most recent Credentialing_Case__c.Status__c != 'Active',
When the AI evaluates the claim,
Then the recommended action is 'Request Additional Documentation' and the rationale references both COB complexity and provider credentialing gap.

Given the Claims Operations Manager clicks 'Accept Recommendation' on the next-best-action card,
When the action is accepted,
Then the system creates the appropriate follow-up record (Task, Case, or SIU_Referral__c) and logs the AI recommendation acceptance to Agentforce_Action_Log__c with fields: Action_Type__c, Confidence_Score__c, Accepted_By__c, Accepted_DateTime__c, Claim__c.

Given the Claims Operations Manager clicks 'Dismiss' on the next-best-action card,
When the dismissal is recorded,
Then Agentforce_Action_Log__c.Dismissed__c = true and Dismissed_Reason__c is captured via a required picklist before dismissal completes (values: 'Already Actioned', 'Incorrect Recommendation', 'Duplicate Alert', 'Other').

Given a Claim__c record where Claim__c.Total_Billed_Amount__c < 500 AND no SIU or COB flags are present,
When the AI evaluates the claim,
Then the recommended action is 'Auto-Adjudicate' and the confidence score reflects the low-complexity profile.

## Solution

Leverage Salesforce Agentforce Einstein Next Best Action framework with a custom Recommendation Strategy built in Strategy Builder. Define a custom Recommendation__c strategy (ClaimsFNOLInterventionStrategy) that ingests Claim__c fields, related Claim_Line__c count (via aggregate), SIU_Flag__c, COB_Indicator__c, and a callout to CredentialingStatusService (Apex) for real-time provider credentialing lookup. Train the recommendation model using Einstein Prediction Builder on historical claim resolution data (6 months of adjudicated claims). Surface recommendations via a custom LWC (claimsNextBestActionPanel) embedded in the Claim__c Lightning Record Page, consuming the NBA REST API. Log all interactions to Agentforce_Action_Log__c via a Record-Triggered Flow. Configure Claim_SLA_Config__mdt to allow operations managers to adjust SLA thresholds without code deployment.
