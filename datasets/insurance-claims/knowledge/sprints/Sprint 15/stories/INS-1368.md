## Description

As a Provider Relations Specialist, I need a consolidated Provider 360 FlexCard on the Provider__c (Person Account) record page that surfaces the provider's active Provider_Network_Agreement__c (PNA), associated Fee_Schedule__c rates, and credentialing status from Credentialing_Case__c, so that I can quickly validate network participation and applicable reimbursement rates during claims adjudication without navigating to multiple related lists.

UAT feedback from Phase 2 testers indicated that adjudicators were spending an average of 4+ minutes per claim locating PNA and fee schedule data across separate tabs, causing throughput bottlenecks.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Provider__c record page is loaded by a user with the 'Provider_Relations' Permission Set,
When the Provider 360 FlexCard renders,
Then it shall display the active Provider_Network_Agreement__c record where PNA__c.Status__c = 'Active' and PNA__c.Effective_Date__c <= TODAY() and PNA__c.Termination_Date__c >= TODAY() or is null.

Given the active PNA is identified,
When the FlexCard renders the Fee Schedule section,
Then Fee_Schedule__c.Base_Rate__c, Fee_Schedule__c.CPT_Code__c, and Fee_Schedule__c.Modifier__c for up to the 10 most frequently billed CPT codes shall be displayed in a sortable datatable.

Given a Provider__c record has an open Credentialing_Case__c,
When the FlexCard loads,
Then Credentialing_Case__c.Status__c and Credentialing_Case__c.Expiration_Date__c shall appear in the 'Credentialing Status' card section with a visual indicator (green/amber/red) based on expiration proximity (>90 days = green, 31-90 days = amber, <=30 days = red).

Given a Provider__c has no active PNA,
When the FlexCard renders,
Then an inline warning banner shall display: 'No Active Network Agreement Found — Out-of-Network rates may apply' and the Fee Schedule section shall be hidden.

-Given the FlexCard is accessed from a Claim__c record, it shall open in a modal overlay.-
(Requirement moved to INS-1372 as part of Claims context panel redesign.)

## Solution

Build OmniStudio FlexCard (FC_Provider360) with three child cards: NetworkAgreement, FeeSchedule, and CredentialingStatus. DataRaptor Extract (DR_GetActivePNA) queries Provider_Network_Agreement__c with date-range SOQL filters. A second DataRaptor Extract (DR_GetFeeScheduleTopCPT) uses an aggregated query on Fee_Schedule__c joined via PNA__c.Id. Credentialing status DataRaptor (DR_GetCredentialingStatus) queries Credentialing_Case__c ordered by CreatedDate DESC LIMIT 1. Conditional visibility rules on FlexCard child cards handle the no-PNA warning state. FlexCard deployed to Provider__c Lightning Record Page via App Builder. Caching strategy: DataRaptor results cached for 5 minutes using OmniStudio cache settings to reduce SOQL query volume.
