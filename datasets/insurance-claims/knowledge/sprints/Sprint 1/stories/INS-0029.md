## Description

As a Claims Operations Manager, I need a Policyholder 360 view on the policyholder's Account record page that consolidates their active Policy__c records, open and recent Claim__c activity, COB (Coordination of Benefits) flags, and EOB delivery preferences so that claims staff can quickly assess a member's full coverage picture during FNOL intake or claim adjudication without switching between multiple systems or record types.

Depends on INS-0023 (core objects) and INS-0024 (permission model).

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Claims Adjudicator or Claims Operations Manager opens a policyholder Account record, when the Policyholder 360 FlexCard renders, then:

1. The Policy Summary section must display all related Policy__c records where Policy__c.Status__c IN ('Active','Pending') showing: Policy__c.Policy_Number__c, Policy__c.LOB__c, Policy__c.Effective_Date__c, Policy__c.Termination_Date__c, and a 'Days Until Expiry' calculated field (Formula: Policy__c.Termination_Date__c - TODAY()).

2. Given the policyholder has COB coverage, when a Policy__c record has Policy__c.COB_Flag__c = true, then the FlexCard must display a prominent amber 'COB Active' banner and show Policy__c.Primary_Carrier__c, Policy__c.Secondary_Carrier__c fields.

3. Given the policyholder has Claim__c records, when the Claims Activity section loads, then it must show the 5 most recent claims sorted by Claim__c.Date_of_Service__c DESC, displaying: Claim__c.Claim_Number__c, Claim__c.Status__c, Claim__c.Total_Billed_Amount__c, Claim__c.Total_Paid_Amount__c, and a hyperlink to the Claim__c record.

4. Given a Claim__c exists with Status__c = 'FNOL_Received' and is less than 24 hours old, when the FlexCard renders, then a 'New FNOL' badge must appear in the Claims Activity section with the claim's creation timestamp.

5. Given the EOB Preferences section renders, when it displays, then it must show the policyholder's Account.EOB_Delivery_Method__c (Picklist: Mail|Email|Portal) and Account.EOB_Email__c with an inline edit capability for Claims staff with Edit permission on Account.

6. -Given the FlexCard includes a premium payment history tile showing last 6 months of premium transactions- (Descoped — premium payment data not available in Phase 1; deferred to Phase 3 integration with billing system).

## Solution

Build OmniStudio FlexCard 'FC_Policyholder360' composed of three child FlexCards: 'FC_Policy_Summary', 'FC_Claims_Activity', 'FC_EOB_Preferences'. DataRaptor Extract 'DR_Policyholder_Policies' queries Policy__c with filter Account__c = :recordId and Status__c IN ('Active','Pending'). DataRaptor Turbo Extract 'DR_Recent_Claims' queries Claim__c ORDER BY Date_of_Service__c DESC LIMIT 5. Conditional block on FC_Policy_Summary uses FlexCard State to show/hide COB banner based on COB_Flag__c value. FNOL badge uses FlexCard conditional rendering: CreatedDate >= (NOW - 86400 seconds). EOB inline edit implemented via OmniScript mini-form launched from FlexCard action button 'Edit EOB Preferences'. Embed on Account Lightning Record Page using dynamic page targeting Account.RecordType.Name = 'Policyholder'.
