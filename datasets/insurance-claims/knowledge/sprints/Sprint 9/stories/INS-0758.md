## Description

**Bug Report — Coverage History Timeline (INS-0756)**

**Environment:** Acme Insurance Full Sandbox (ACME_FS1)
**Severity:** High — affects data integrity display for QA sign-off

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Navigate to any Policyholder (Person Account) record that has at least one Policy__c record where Policy__c.LOB__c contains an ampersand or slash character (e.g., 'Health & Dental' or 'Auto/Home').
2. Open the Coverage History Timeline LWC component on the record page.
3. Observe the timeline rendering.

**Expected Behavior:** Each Policy__c record appears exactly once on the timeline regardless of the LOB__c field value.

**Actual Behavior:** Policies with LOB__c values containing '&' or '/' characters are rendered twice on the timeline. The duplicate entry appears immediately after the original with identical dates but a blank Policy__c.Name field.

**Root Cause Hypothesis:** The Apex controller 'CoverageHistoryController.cls' getCoverageHistory() method likely does not encode LOB__c values before passing them to the LWC wrapper, causing the JSON serialization to split on special characters and produce malformed wrapper objects that the LWC iterates twice.

**Impacted Records:** 47 policyholders identified in ACME_FS1 with affected LOB__c values.

**Attachments:** Screenshot_DuplicateTimeline.png, DebugLog_CoverageHistory_20240918.txt

## Acceptance Criteria

As per Description. Fix verified when: no duplicate Policy__c entries appear on the timeline for any LOB__c value including special characters ('&', '/', '%', '#'). Regression test must pass for standard LOB__c values ('Medical', 'Dental', 'Vision', 'Auto').

## Solution

Investigate CoverageHistoryController.cls getCoverageHistory() Apex method — apply JSON.serialize() encoding to the wrapper list before returning to LWC. Review LWC coverageHistoryTimeline JS controller for any string split logic operating on LOB__c. Add Apex unit test cases covering LOB__c = 'Health & Dental' and 'Auto/Home'. Deploy fix to ACME_FS1 and execute full regression on Coverage History component.
