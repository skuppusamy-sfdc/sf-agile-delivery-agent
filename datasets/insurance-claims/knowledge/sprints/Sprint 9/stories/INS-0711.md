## Description

**Bug Report**

The coverage history gap detection logic introduced in INS-0708 is incorrectly flagging same-day plan transitions as coverage gaps, causing false 'Coverage Gap' indicators to appear on the Policy__c timeline for members who switched plans without any lapse.

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Navigate to any Policy__c record where a member transitioned from one plan to another on the same calendar date (Policy_Coverage_History__c.End_Date__c of record A = Policy_Coverage_History__c.Effective_Date__c of record B).
2. Open the Coverage History Timeline LWC component on the record page.
3. Observe the timeline rendering between the two adjacent history nodes.

**Expected Behavior:**
No 'Coverage Gap' indicator should appear between two Policy_Coverage_History__c records when the End_Date__c of the earlier record equals the Effective_Date__c of the later record (same-day transition = continuous coverage).

**Actual Behavior:**
A red 'Coverage Gap – 0 days' or occasionally 'Coverage Gap – 1 day' indicator is displayed between same-day transition records, causing confusion for Claims Operations Managers and potentially triggering incorrect retroactive claim denials.

**Environment:** Acme Insurance – Full Sandbox (FS1)
**Affected Records:** ~340 Policy__c records identified via SOQL audit
**Regression:** Introduced in Sprint 9 deployment on 2024-11-04

## Solution

Fix the gap detection Apex method 'getCoverageHistory' in the 'CoverageHistoryController' class. The current comparison uses a strict less-than operator (<) when evaluating date differences; update logic to treat a gap as valid only when the difference between consecutive records' End_Date__c and Effective_Date__c is strictly greater than 1 day (accounting for same-day and next-day contiguous transitions per NCQA continuous coverage standards). Update the SOQL sort to ORDER BY Effective_Date__c ASC to ensure consistent ordering before gap calculation. Add a dedicated test method 'testSameDayTransitionNoGap' in CoverageHistoryControllerTest covering same-day, next-day, and 2+ day gap scenarios. Deploy hotfix to FS1 for QA validation before promoting to UAT.
