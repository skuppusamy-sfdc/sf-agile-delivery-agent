## Description

As a Compliance Officer, I need an approval workflow on Payment__c records where Payment__c.Amount__c exceeds $10,000 to require a senior Claims Operations Manager sign-off before disbursement is initiated, so that large payments are subject to a secondary review control that reduces financial risk and satisfies internal audit requirements for high-value disbursements.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Payment__c record is created or updated where Payment__c.Amount__c > 10000 and Payment__c.Status__c = 'Pending',
When the record is saved,
Then the Salesforce Approval Process 'High_Value_Payment_Approval' is automatically submitted, Payment__c.Status__c is set to 'Pending Approval', and an approval request notification is sent to the assigned approver in the 'Senior Claims Approver' Public Group.

Given the approval request is submitted,
When the approver approves the Payment__c record,
Then Payment__c.Status__c transitions to 'Approved', Payment__c.Approved_By__c is stamped with the approver's User.Name, Payment__c.Approval_Date__c = TODAY(), and the downstream EFT/check disbursement process is triggered.

Given the approver rejects the Payment__c record,
When rejection is submitted with a comment,
Then Payment__c.Status__c = 'Rejected', Payment__c.Rejection_Reason__c is populated from the approval comment, and a Task is created for the originating Claims Operations Manager to review.

Given Payment__c.Amount__c <= 10000,
When the Payment__c record is created,
Then no approval process is triggered and Payment__c.Status__c remains 'Pending' for standard processing.

Given a Payment__c record in 'Pending Approval' status,
When 48 business hours elapse without approver action,
Then an escalation email is sent to the Claims Operations Manager group and the approval is re-assigned to the secondary approver defined in the Approval Process escalation step.

## Solution

Configure a Salesforce Approval Process ('High_Value_Payment_Approval') on Payment__c with entry criteria Amount__c > 10000 and Status__c = 'Pending'. Initial submission via a Record-Triggered Flow (After Insert/Update) that calls the Process.ProcessWorkitemRequest Apex method to auto-submit. Approval step assigned to 'Senior Claims Approver' Public Group using 'Unanimous' approval requirement. Approval/rejection final actions use Field Updates for Status__c, Approved_By__c, Approval_Date__c and a Task creation action. Escalation configured at 48-hour step timeout with re-assignment to secondary approver. Payment__c.Approval_Date__c is a Date field; approver stamp uses a Field Update pointing to {!$User.Name}. Audit trail captured via Salesforce standard Approval History related list.
