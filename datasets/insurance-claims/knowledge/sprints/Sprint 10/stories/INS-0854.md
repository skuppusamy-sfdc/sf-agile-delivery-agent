## Description

**Bug Report**

**Environment:** Full Sandbox (Acme-FS2)
**Discovered:** Sprint 10 Day 5, during PCN compliance report validation

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Trigger the bulk PCN send job by advancing a Network_Termination_Case__c to NTC.Status__c = 'In Review' with at least 3 affiliated Group_Service_Agreement__c (GSA) records.
2. The batch Apex job 'PCNBulkSendBatch' executes and sends email notifications to all GSA contacts.
3. Navigate to the Provider_Change_Notification__c record linked to the NTC.
4. Observe PCN.Recipient_Count__c field value.

**Expected Result:** PCN.Recipient_Count__c reflects the total number of unique recipients who received the notification email (e.g., 3 for 3 GSA contacts).

**Actual Result:** PCN.Recipient_Count__c = 0 on all PCN records regardless of how many recipients were processed. The emails are confirmed as delivered (visible in Email Logs), so the send itself is not failing.

**Root Cause Hypothesis:** The 'PCNBulkSendBatch' finish() method constructs the PCN update DML statement but references a local variable 'recipientCount' that is declared inside the execute() scope and is not accumulated into a class-level instance variable. The finish() method therefore always writes 0.

**Impact:** Medium — compliance reports querying PCN.Recipient_Count__c for regulatory filing show incorrect zero values, requiring manual reconciliation.

## Acceptance Criteria

As per Description. Fix verified when PCN.Recipient_Count__c accurately reflects the sum of all unique recipients processed across all execute() batches after the finish() method completes. Verified in sandbox with test scenarios of 1, 50, and 201 recipient records.

## Solution

Refactor 'PCNBulkSendBatch' Apex class to declare a class-level instance variable 'Integer totalRecipientCount = 0;' and increment it within each execute() method invocation using 'totalRecipientCount += recipientList.size();'. In the finish() method, perform a DML update on the PCN record setting PCN.Recipient_Count__c = totalRecipientCount. Note: because Batch Apex re-instantiates the class between execute() chunks, refactor to use a stateful batch (implement Database.Stateful interface) to preserve the counter across chunks. Add a unit test asserting PCN.Recipient_Count__c > 0 after batch execution with a mock recipient list of 5 records.
