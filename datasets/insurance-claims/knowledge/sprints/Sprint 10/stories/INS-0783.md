# INS-0783: For-cause provider termination: auto-suspend active Claim__c payments and trigger SIU referral workflow

- **Sprint**: Acme Insurance Sprint 10 (Phase 2)
- **Status**: In Progress
- **Type**: Story
- **Priority**: High
- **Resolution**: Unresolved
- **Assignee**: Kevin Nguyen
- **Reporter**: Lisa Park

- **Epic**: Provider Termination
- **Story Points**: 8
- **Labels**: Phase2, ForCause, SIU, PaymentSuspension, ProviderTermination

## Description

As a Claims Operations Manager, I need the system to automatically suspend all pending Payment__c disbursements and open Claim__c adjudication for a provider when a Network_Termination_Case__c is created with Termination_Case.Reason__c = 'For Cause', and to initiate an SIU referral workflow, so that Acme Insurance prevents potentially fraudulent or improper payments from being disbursed during a for-cause investigation and meets its internal fraud prevention SLA of suspending payments within 2 hours of a for-cause termination being logged. Complements INS-0778 (in-network repricing block).

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Network_Termination_Case__c is inserted with Termination_Case.Reason__c = 'For Cause' and Termination_Case.Provider__c populated,
When the record is saved,
Then all Payment__c records where Payment.Payee_Provider__c = terminating provider and Payment.Status__c IN ('Approved', 'Scheduled') are updated to Payment.Status__c = 'Suspended – For Cause' and Payment.Suspension_Reason__c = 'Provider For Cause Termination – ' + Termination_Case.Case_Number__c within 2 hours (async processing SLA).

Given Payment__c records are suspended,
When suspension is applied,
Then the total count and dollar value of suspended payments are written back to Termination_Case.Suspended_Payment_Count__c and Termination_Case.Suspended_Payment_Amount__c.

Given a for-cause Network_Termination_Case__c is created,
When the record is inserted,
Then all Claim__c records where Claim.Rendering_Provider__c = terminating provider and Claim.Status__c IN ('In Adjudication', 'Pended') are updated to Claim.Status__c = 'Suspended – For Cause Investigation' and a Claim_Flag__c record is created per claim with Flag_Type__c = 'For Cause Provider – SIU Review Required'.

Given Claim__c and Payment__c suspensions are applied,
When the suspensions complete,
Then a Platform Event 'SIU_Referral_Event__e' is published with fields: Provider_NPI__c, Termination_Case_Id__c, Suspended_Claim_Count__c, Suspended_Payment_Amount__c, and Referral_Priority__c = 'Urgent'.

Given the SIU_Referral_Event__e is published,
When the SIU subscriber Flow receives the event,
Then a Case of RecordType 'SIU Investigation' is created with Case.Subject = 'For Cause Provider Termination – [NPI]', Case.Priority = 'High', Case.Origin = 'Automated – Network Termination', and the case is assigned to the SIU queue.

Given the SIU Case is created,
When the case is saved,
Then a DocuSign envelope is generated using the 'SIU_Provider_Notification' template and sent to Provider.Legal_Contact_Email__c, and Termination_Case.DocuSign_Status__c = 'SIU Notice Sent'.

Given a Compliance Officer reviews the for-cause termination and sets Termination_Case.Investigation_Outcome__c = 'Cleared – Reinstate Provider',
When the field is updated,
Then all Payment__c records with Payment.Suspension_Reason__c containing the case number are reverted to their prior status, Claim__c records are returned to 'In Adjudication', and Credentialing_Case__c records on hold (per INS-0781 logic) are also released.

## Solution

Implement a Record-Triggered Flow on Network_Termination_Case__c (after insert, entry criteria: Reason__c = 'For Cause') that publishes a Platform Event 'ForCause_Termination_Event__e'. A Platform Event-triggered Apex handler 'ForCauseTerminationHandler' processes Payment__c and Claim__c suspensions in bulkified SOQL/DML loops using a Queueable chain to respect governor limits and meet the 2-hour SLA (Queueable depth: Payment suspension → Claim suspension → SIU event publish). Store pre-suspension Payment.Status__c in Payment.Pre_Suspension_Status__c for rollback capability. The SIU referral subscriber is a separate Platform Event-triggered Flow consuming 'SIU_Referral_Event__e', creating the SIU Case and triggering DocuSign via the OmniStudio DocuSign Integration Action. Reinstatement path handled by a Record-Triggered Flow on Network_Termination_Case__c (after update, entry criteria: Investigation_Outcome__c = 'Cleared – Reinstate Provider') calling an Apex invocable 'ProviderReinstatementAction'. All actions logged to a custom Termination_Audit_Log__c object for compliance reporting.
