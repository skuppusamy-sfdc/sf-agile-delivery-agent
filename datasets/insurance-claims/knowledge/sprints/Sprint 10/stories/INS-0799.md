## Description

**Bug Report**

**Summary:** When the Fee_Schedule__c deactivation flow (implemented in INS-0797) is triggered by a Network_Termination_Case__c closure, the Fee_Schedule__c.Deactivation_Date__c field is being set to the current system date (TODAY) instead of NTC.Termination_Effective_Date__c, causing incorrect retroactive or premature deactivation dates on fee schedule records and downstream payment calculation errors.

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Create a Network_Termination_Case__c record with NTC.Termination_Effective_Date__c = a future date (e.g., 30 days from today).
2. Link one or more Fee_Schedule__c records to the associated PNA.
3. Update NTC.Status__c = 'Closed' to trigger the deactivation Record-Triggered Flow.
4. Inspect the updated Fee_Schedule__c records.
5. Observe that Fee_Schedule__c.Deactivation_Date__c = TODAY (date of flow execution) rather than the NTC.Termination_Effective_Date__c value.

**Expected Behavior:** Fee_Schedule__c.Deactivation_Date__c should equal NTC.Termination_Effective_Date__c as specified in the INS-0797 acceptance criteria.

**Actual Behavior:** Fee_Schedule__c.Deactivation_Date__c = Date.today() at the time of flow execution.

**Root Cause (Preliminary):** In the Record-Triggered Flow on Network_Termination_Case__c, the Update Records element for Fee_Schedule__c is using a formula of `TODAY()` for the Deactivation_Date__c field instead of referencing the flow variable {!$Record.Termination_Effective_Date__c}. This was likely introduced during a copy-paste of a similar flow element from an earlier deactivation flow.

**Impact:** High — incorrect deactivation dates cause the PaymentEngine to apply out-of-network rates for claim lines with service dates between the incorrect deactivation date and the actual termination effective date, resulting in underpayment or overpayment depending on the direction of the date error.

**Environment:** Acme Insurance Full Sandbox (ACME_FS1) and confirmed in ACME_UAT1.
**Regression:** Introduced in Sprint 10 initial deployment on 2024-12-02.

## Acceptance Criteria

As per Description. After fix: Fee_Schedule__c.Deactivation_Date__c must equal NTC.Termination_Effective_Date__c for all fee schedules deactivated via the NTC closure flow. Verified via a bulk test scenario with 10+ fee schedule records across 3 NTC records with distinct Termination_Effective_Date__c values.

## Solution

Open the Record-Triggered Flow 'NTCClosure_FeeScheduleDeactivation_Flow' in Flow Builder. Locate the Update Records element targeting Fee_Schedule__c. Change the Deactivation_Date__c field assignment from the hardcoded formula TODAY() to the flow resource reference {!$Record.Termination_Effective_Date__c}. Verify that the flow variable is correctly mapped from the triggering NTC record. Save and activate the corrected flow version. Run Apex unit tests in ClaimLineTerminationBlockHandlerTest and FeeScheduleDeactivationTest classes to confirm no regressions. Deploy corrected flow to ACME_FS1, execute bulk regression test, then promote to ACME_UAT1 and production via change set.
