## Description

**Bug Report**

**Summary:** The PCN recipient generation logic introduced in INS-0955 only queries Provider_Network_Agreement__c records where PNA.Status__c = 'Active'. Providers whose PNAs are in 'Pending Renewal' status also have contractual entitlement to rate change notices, as their existing contract terms remain in force during the renewal window. These providers are silently omitted from PCN_Recipient__c records.

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Set a Provider_Network_Agreement__c record to Status__c = 'Pending Renewal' for Provider NPI 1234567890.
2. Publish a Fee_Schedule__c record linked to that PNA.
3. Observe PCN_Recipient__c records created — Provider NPI 1234567890 is absent.
4. Cross-reference with a PNA where Status__c = 'Active' — that provider IS included.

**Expected Result:** PCN_Recipient__c records are created for all PNA records with Status__c IN ('Active', 'Pending Renewal') that reference the published Fee_Schedule__c.

**Actual Result:** Only PNA records with Status__c = 'Active' generate PCN_Recipient__c records.

**Environment:** Production (discovered during compliance audit)
**Frequency:** Reproducible 100% for 'Pending Renewal' PNAs
**Compliance Risk:** HIGH — state regulations in CA, TX, and NY require 30-day advance notice to ALL contracted providers regardless of renewal status. Legal has flagged this as a potential regulatory violation.

## Acceptance Criteria

As per Description. The SOQL in PCNGenerationService.createPCN() must be updated to use PNA.Status__c IN ('Active', 'Pending Renewal'). A retroactive job must be run to identify any PCNs distributed in the last 90 days that are missing 'Pending Renewal' recipients and generate supplemental PCN_Recipient__c records with PCN.Supplemental_Flag__c = TRUE.

## Solution

Update the SOQL WHERE clause in PCNGenerationService.createPCN() Apex class from 'Status__c = Active' to 'Status__c IN (Active, Pending Renewal)'. Write a one-time Apex batch job 'PCNRetroactiveRecipientBatch' to identify PCNs from the past 90 days missing Pending Renewal recipients and create supplemental PCN_Recipient__c records. Coordinate with Compliance Officer (Amanda Foster) to review supplemental notices before distribution. Add a new test method 'testPendingRenewalPNAIncluded()' to PCNGenerationServiceTest. Deploy hotfix to Production after UAT sign-off from Provider Relations lead.
