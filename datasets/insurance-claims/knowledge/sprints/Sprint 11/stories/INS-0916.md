## Description

As a Provider Relations Specialist, I need the system to automatically generate a Provider_Change_Notification__c (PCN) record and deliver an email notification to affected in-network providers whenever a Fee_Schedule__c rate change is published so that providers receive timely, compliant advance notice of reimbursement rate changes as required by their Provider_Network_Agreement__c terms. Extends INS-0885.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Fee_Schedule__c record is saved with Status__c = 'Published' and a future Effective_Date__c,
When the effective date is 30 or fewer days from today,
Then a Provider_Change_Notification__c record must be created with PCN.Type__c = 'Rate Change', PCN.Status__c = 'Pending Delivery', and PCN.Related_Fee_Schedule__c linked to the triggering Fee_Schedule__c.

Given the PCN record is created,
When the system identifies affected providers,
Then all Provider__c (Person Account) records linked via active Provider_Network_Agreement__c.Status__c = 'Active' whose PNA covers the impacted CPT codes and LOB must be associated to the PCN via PCN_Provider_Junction__c.

Given PCN_Provider_Junction__c records are populated,
When the notification batch job executes,
Then each provider must receive an email to Provider__c.NPI_Email__c using the 'Rate_Change_Notification' email template, including old allowed amount, new allowed amount, effective date, and CPT codes impacted.

Given an email is successfully dispatched,
When the messaging service confirms delivery,
Then PCN.Status__c must update to 'Delivered' and PCN.Delivery_Timestamp__c must be stamped.

Given a provider has opted out of email notifications (Provider__c.Notification_Opt_Out__c = true),
When the batch job processes that provider,
Then the system must skip email delivery, log a PCN_Provider_Junction__c.Delivery_Status__c = 'Opted Out', and flag for manual outreach assignment to the owning Provider Relations Specialist.

-Given PCN delivery fails after 1 retry, escalate to Claims Operations Manager queue.- (Updated: escalate to Provider Relations Specialist queue per compliance review.)

Given PCN.Status__c = 'Delivered',
When 30 days have elapsed without provider acknowledgement,
Then PCN.Status__c must auto-update to 'Acknowledged – No Response' and a Task must be created on the Provider__c record assigned to the owning Provider Relations Specialist.

## Solution

Record-Triggered Flow on Fee_Schedule__c fires on update when Status__c changes to 'Published'. Flow invokes Apex batch class (PCNGenerationBatch.cls) via a Platform Event (Fee_Schedule_Published__e) to decouple processing. The batch queries active PNAs and creates PCN and PCN_Provider_Junction__c records in bulk. Email delivery handled by Apex EmailService leveraging Salesforce Email Templates with merge fields populated via DataRaptor Extract (OmniStudio) for provider-specific rate data. A scheduled Flow runs nightly to check 30-day acknowledgement SLA and auto-update PCN status. Custom metadata (PCN_Config__mdt) stores retry limits and escalation queue IDs. All PCN records governed by Sharing Rule based on Provider Relations Specialist ownership.
