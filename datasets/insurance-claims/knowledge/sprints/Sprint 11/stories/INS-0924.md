## Description

As a Provider Relations Specialist, I need an automated workflow that generates a Provider_Change_Notification__c (PCN) record and sends a templated email to the contracted provider whenever a Fee_Schedule__c rate is modified on an active Provider_Network_Agreement__c, so that providers receive timely, compliant notice of rate changes as required by state mandate and our GSA obligations. Extends INS-0893 (PNA lifecycle management).

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Fee_Schedule__c record linked to an active Provider_Network_Agreement__c is updated (any change to Fee_Schedule_Line__c.Allowed_Amount__c or Fee_Schedule_Line__c.Rate_Percentage__c),
When the record is saved,
Then a Provider_Change_Notification__c is created with PCN.Type__c = 'Rate Change', PCN.Status__c = 'Pending Delivery', PCN.Effective_Date__c = Fee_Schedule__c.Effective_Start_Date__c, and PCN.PNA__c populated with the parent PNA Id.

Given the PCN record is created,
When PCN.Status__c = 'Pending Delivery',
Then an email is dispatched within 15 minutes using the 'Rate_Change_Notification' Email Template to the Provider__c.NPI_Email__c address and CC'd to the assigned Provider Relations Specialist.

Given the provider is a Facility (Business Account) rather than a Person Account,
When the PCN is generated,
Then the email recipient is resolved from Facility__c.Billing_Contact_Email__c and PCN.Recipient_Type__c = 'Facility'.

Given the email is successfully delivered,
When the Email Log confirms delivery,
Then PCN.Status__c transitions to 'Delivered' and PCN.Delivered_Date__c is stamped.

Given PCN.Delivered_Date__c is null after 24 hours,
When the scheduled job runs,
Then a follow-up Task is created on the Provider__c record assigned to the Provider Relations Specialist with Subject = 'PCN Delivery Failure - Manual Follow-Up Required'.

## Solution

Record-Triggered Flow on Fee_Schedule_Line__c (after save, updated fields: Allowed_Amount__c, Rate_Percentage__c) invokes an Apex action 'PCNGeneratorService.createRateChangePCN()'. The service traverses the Fee_Schedule__c -> Provider_Network_Agreement__c -> Provider__c/Facility__c relationship chain to resolve recipient. OrgWide Email Address used for outbound send via Messaging.sendEmail(). A Scheduled Apex job 'PCNDeliveryMonitor' runs hourly to detect undelivered PCNs and create follow-up Tasks. PCN.Status__c managed via a Status field with validation rules preventing backward transitions.
