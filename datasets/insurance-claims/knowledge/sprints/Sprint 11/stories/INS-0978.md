## Description

As a Provider Relations Specialist, I need an automated notification workflow that generates a Provider_Change_Notification__c (PCN) record and sends a templated email whenever a Fee_Schedule__c record is created or its Allowed_Rate__c values are materially modified (>5% variance), so that affected providers and internal stakeholders are informed of rate changes within the contractually required 30-day advance notice window.

This story covers the notification generation layer; the provider-facing portal display of PCNs is handled in INS-0981.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Fee_Schedule__c record is saved with Status__c transitioning from 'Draft' to 'Approved',
When the Effective_Date__c is more than 0 days in the future,
Then a Provider_Change_Notification__c record must be created with PCN.Type__c = 'Rate Change', PCN.Fee_Schedule__c = the triggering record ID, PCN.Notice_Date__c = TODAY(), PCN.Required_Notice_By__c = Fee_Schedule__c.Effective_Date__c - 30 days, and PCN.Status__c = 'Pending Delivery'.

Given a PCN record is created with Status__c = 'Pending Delivery',
When PCN.Required_Notice_By__c is within 5 business days,
Then an escalation email must be sent to the Provider Relations Specialist assigned to the related Provider_Network_Agreement__c.Owner using the 'PCN_Escalation_Alert' email template, and PCN.Escalation_Sent__c must be set to TRUE.

Given a Fee_Schedule_Line__c child record is updated where ABS((New_Allowed_Rate__c - Old_Allowed_Rate__c) / Old_Allowed_Rate__c) > 0.05,
When the parent Fee_Schedule__c.Status__c = 'Active',
Then a new PCN with PCN.Type__c = 'Mid-Term Rate Adjustment' must be generated and PCN.Rate_Variance_Pct__c must be populated with the calculated percentage.

Given a PCN is successfully delivered via email,
When the email send action completes without error,
Then PCN.Status__c must be updated to 'Delivered' and PCN.Delivery_Timestamp__c must be stamped.

-Given the provider has opted out of email notifications (Provider__c.Email_Opt_Out__c = TRUE), suppress the email but still create the PCN record.- (Superseded: Opt-out does not apply to contractually mandated rate change notices per Legal guidance LG-2024-089. Email must always be sent.)

## Solution

Record-Triggered Flow on Fee_Schedule__c (after save, trigger on Status__c change to 'Approved') calls an Apex invocable action 'PCNGeneratorService' that creates Provider_Change_Notification__c records in bulk for all linked PNA providers. A separate scheduled Flow ('PCN Escalation Checker') runs nightly to evaluate PCN.Required_Notice_By__c proximity and fires escalation emails via OrgWideEmailAddress. Email templates managed in Salesforce Classic Email Templates folder 'PCN_Templates'. Rate variance calculation on Fee_Schedule_Line__c handled by a before-update Apex Trigger that stores Old_Allowed_Rate__c in a shadow field prior to overwrite. All PCN creation events published as Platform Events (PCN_Created__e) for downstream ComplianceReporting consumption.
