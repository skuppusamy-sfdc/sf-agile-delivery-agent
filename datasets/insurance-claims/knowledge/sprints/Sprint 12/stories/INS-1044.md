## Description

As a licensed broker, I need to submit and track commission disputes directly through the Agent Portal so that discrepancies in Payment__c records are formally logged, routed to the Broker Relations Coordinator, and resolved with full audit trail without requiring phone or email escalation. Extends INS-1043 by linking dispute cases back to the Commission Dashboard.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a broker identifies an incorrect commission on the INS-1043 Commission Dashboard,
When they click 'Dispute This Payment' on a Payment__c row,
Then an OmniScript dispute form opens pre-populated with Payment__c.Id, Payment__c.Commission_Amount__c, and Policy__c.Policy_Number__c.

Given the broker completes the dispute form with Dispute_Reason__c (picklist: 'Incorrect Rate', 'Missing Policy', 'Chargeback Error', 'Other') and a free-text description,
When they submit,
Then a new Case record is created with: RecordType = 'Commission_Dispute', Status = 'New', Priority = 'High', Origin = 'Agent Portal', and a lookup to the disputed Payment__c.Id.

Given the Case is created,
When the Record-Triggered Flow fires on Case insert,
Then the assigned Broker Relations Coordinator queue receives an email notification and the Case appears in their 'Commission Disputes' list view within 2 minutes.

Given the broker returns to the portal,
When they navigate to 'My Disputes',
Then a FlexCard lists all their open and closed Case records with RecordType = 'Commission_Dispute', showing Case.Status, Case.CreatedDate, Case.Resolution_Notes__c, and linked Payment__c.Commission_Amount__c.

Given a Broker Relations Coordinator resolves the dispute and sets Case.Status = 'Closed - Resolved',
When the record is saved,
Then the broker receives an automated email (via Record-Triggered Flow email alert) containing the resolution summary from Case.Resolution_Notes__c and the updated Payment__c.Commission_Amount__c value.

## Solution

OmniScript ('CommissionDisputeOS') launched via FlexCard action from INS-1043 dashboard. DataRaptor Load creates the Case record with all required field mappings. Record-Triggered Flow on Case object (trigger: after insert, RecordType = 'Commission_Dispute') sends email alert to Broker Relations Coordinator queue and sets Case.SLA_Due_Date__c = TODAY() + 5 business days via custom formula invocation. 'My Disputes' FlexCard uses DataRaptor Extract on Case filtered by AccountId of logged-in user's linked broker Account. A second Record-Triggered Flow on Case update (Status changed to 'Closed - Resolved') fires email alert to broker contact email. Permission Set 'Agent_Portal_Dispute_User' grants Create on Case, Read on Payment__c.
