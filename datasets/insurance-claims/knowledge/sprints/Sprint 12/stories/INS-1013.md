## Description

As a Broker Relations Coordinator, I need the system to automatically calculate and apply tiered commission rates to new and renewed Policy__c records based on the producing agent's active Group Service Agreement (GSA) so that commission Payment__c records are generated accurately without manual data entry, reducing errors and disputes.

Tiered rates are defined on Group_Service_Agreement__c.Commission_Tier__c (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum) and associated Commission_Rate_Schedule__c child records that specify rate percentages by LOB and premium band.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Policy__c record is created or renewed with a producing Agent__c linked to an active Group_Service_Agreement__c,
When Policy__c.Status__c transitions to 'Active',
Then an Apex trigger evaluates the agent's GSA tier and premium band to determine the applicable commission rate from Commission_Rate_Schedule__c.

Given the applicable rate is determined,
When the calculation completes,
Then a Payment__c record is created with Payment__c.Type__c = 'Commission', Payment__c.Amount__c = Policy__c.Annual_Premium__c * applicable_rate, Payment__c.Commission_Status__c = 'Pending Approval', and Payment__c.Policy__c = the triggering policy.

Given a GSA has LOB-specific rate overrides,
When Policy__c.Line_of_Business__c matches an override row on Commission_Rate_Schedule__c,
Then the LOB-specific rate takes precedence over the base tier rate.

Given no active GSA exists for the producing agent,
When the trigger fires,
Then no Payment__c is auto-created, and a Case is opened with Subject = 'Missing GSA — Commission Calculation Blocked' assigned to the Broker Relations queue.

Given a Policy__c is cancelled mid-term,
When Policy__c.Status__c = 'Cancelled' and cancellation is within 90 days of effective date,
Then the linked Payment__c.Commission_Status__c is updated to 'Clawback Pending' and Payment__c.Clawback_Reason__c = 'Policy Cancelled Within 90 Days'.

## Solution

Apex Trigger 'PolicyCommissionTrigger' on Policy__c (after insert, after update) invokes 'CommissionCalculationService' Apex class. Service queries Commission_Rate_Schedule__c with SOQL filtering on GSA tier, LOB, and premium band using indexed fields. Payment__c DML performed in a separate Apex method to respect trigger best practices. Custom Metadata Type 'Commission_Config__mdt' stores clawback window (default 90 days) for configurability. Record-Triggered Flow handles Case creation for missing GSA scenario to keep Apex lean. All calculations covered by Apex unit tests at ≥85% coverage targeting the calculation service class.
