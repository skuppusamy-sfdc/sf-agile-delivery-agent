## Description

As a Compliance Officer, I need a two-level approval process for Commission_Line__c records where the calculated commission amount exceeds $25,000 so that high-value payments receive appropriate financial oversight before disbursement. This story supersedes the approval threshold defined in INS-0983 (threshold raised from $10,000 to $25,000 per Compliance review on 2024-11-14). Level 1 approval is the Broker Relations Manager; Level 2 approval is the VP of Finance.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Commission_Line__c record is created or updated where Commission_Line__c.Calculated_Amount__c > 25000 and Commission_Line__c.Status__c = 'Pending Approval',
When the record is saved,
Then the Salesforce Approval Process 'Commission_High_Value_Approval' is automatically submitted via an Apex Trigger (after insert/after update).

Given the Approval Process is submitted,
When the Level 1 approver (Broker Relations Manager role) receives the approval request,
Then an approval notification email is sent using template 'Commission_L1_Approval_Request' containing Commission_Line__c.Policy__c, Commission_Line__c.Calculated_Amount__c, Commission_Line__c.Commission_Type__c, and a direct link to the record.

Given the Level 1 approver approves the request,
When the approval advances to Level 2,
Then Commission_Line__c.Approval_Stage__c = 'L1 Approved – Awaiting L2' and a notification is sent to the VP of Finance role.

Given both levels approve the Commission_Line__c,
When the final approval is granted,
Then Commission_Line__c.Status__c = 'Approved for Payment' and Commission_Line__c.Final_Approved_Date__c = TODAY().

Given any approver rejects the Commission_Line__c at either level,
When the rejection is submitted with a comment,
Then Commission_Line__c.Status__c = 'Rejected' and Commission_Line__c.Rejection_Reason__c is populated with the approver's comment, and the submitting Broker Relations Coordinator receives an email notification.

Given Commission_Line__c.Calculated_Amount__c <= 25000,
When the record is saved,
Then the approval process is NOT triggered and Commission_Line__c.Status__c remains 'Pending Approval' for standard single-level processing.

## Solution

Configure a standard Salesforce Approval Process ('Commission_High_Value_Approval') with two sequential approval steps: Step 1 assigned to 'Broker Relations Manager' role; Step 2 assigned to 'VP Finance' role. Entry criteria: Commission_Line__c.Calculated_Amount__c > 25000. An Apex Trigger (CommissionLineApprovalTrigger, after insert/after update) calls Approval.process() to auto-submit qualifying records. Commission_Line__c.Approval_Stage__c (picklist) and Commission_Line__c.Final_Approved_Date__c (date) are new fields added to the object. Approval step field updates set Approval_Stage__c at each transition. Email templates created in Classic Email Templates for compatibility with Approval Process notifications. Rejection path uses a final rejection action to update Status__c and populate Rejection_Reason__c via field update action.
