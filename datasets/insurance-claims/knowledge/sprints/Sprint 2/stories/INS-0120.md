## Description

**Bug Report — Policyholder 360 Duplicate Claims**

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Navigate to any Policyholder Person Account record that has 2 or more Policy__c records with Status__c = 'Active'.
2. Observe the 'Open Claims' section of the Policyholder 360 FlexCard (delivered in INS-0116).
3. Note the number of Claim__c records displayed.

**Expected Behavior:**
Each Claim__c record associated with the policyholder should appear exactly once in the Open Claims panel, regardless of how many active Policy__c records are linked to the account.

**Actual Behavior:**
Claim__c records are duplicated — one entry per active Policy__c record. For example, a policyholder with 3 active policies and 2 open claims shows 6 claim rows (2 claims × 3 policies) due to a Cartesian join in the DataRaptor Extract.

**Environment:** Partial Sandbox (PS1)
**Frequency:** 100% reproducible when PolicyCount >= 2
**Severity:** High — Claims Operations Managers are reporting inflated claim counts and incorrect Total_Billed_Amount__c sums in the summary KPI tile.

## Acceptance Criteria

As per Description.

Given a Policyholder has 3 active Policy__c records and 2 open Claim__c records,
When the Open Claims panel of the Policyholder 360 FlexCard renders,
Then exactly 2 Claim__c rows must display — no duplicates.

Given the fix is deployed,
When the KPI summary tile calculates total open claim value,
Then the sum of Claim__c.Total_Billed_Amount__c must equal the true aggregate of distinct Claim__c records, not a multiplied value.

## Solution

Root cause: DataRaptor Extract DR_Extract_OpenClaims joins Claim__c through Policy__c as an intermediate object, producing a Cartesian product. Fix: Refactor DR_Extract_OpenClaims to query Claim__c directly using Claim__c.Policyholder__c = :contextId (Account lookup on Claim__c) rather than traversing the Policy__c relationship. Add DISTINCT equivalent by setting the DataRaptor 'Group By' on Claim__c.Id. Validate in PS1 sandbox with test accounts having 1, 2, 3, and 5 active policies. Update associated DataRaptor unit test records. Regression test INS-0116 FlexCard end-to-end after fix.
