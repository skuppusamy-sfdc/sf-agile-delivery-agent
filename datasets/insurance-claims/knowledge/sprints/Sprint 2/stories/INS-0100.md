## Description

**Bug Report**

**Environment:** Acme Insurance Full Sandbox (FS1)
**Discovered During:** Sprint 2 Provider 360 UAT – INS-0094 testing
**Reported By:** Carlos Rodriguez (Provider Relations Specialist) during UAT walkthrough

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Navigate to a Provider__c (Person Account) record that has at least one Provider_Network_Agreement__c with PNA__c.Status__c = 'Terminated' and at least one with PNA__c.Status__c = 'Active'.
2. Load the Provider 360 FlexCard on the Lightning Record Page.
3. Observe the PNA panel.

**Expected Behavior:** Only PNA records where PNA__c.Status__c = 'Active' are displayed in the PNA panel. Terminated agreements are excluded.

**Actual Behavior:** All PNA records regardless of Status__c are displayed in the PNA panel, including 'Terminated', 'Expired', and 'Draft' records. This causes Provider Network Specialists to incorrectly believe a provider is still in-network under a terminated contract, creating downstream adjudication errors and potential compliance violations.

**Root Cause (Preliminary):** The DR_GetActivePNAs DataRaptor Turbo Extract is querying Provider_Network_Agreement__c without a WHERE clause filter on PNA__c.Status__c. The filter condition was accidentally removed during a DataRaptor merge conflict in the last deployment.

**Impact:** Critical data accuracy issue. Affects all Provider 360 pages — estimated 1,200+ provider records in sandbox. If promoted to production, could cause incorrect in-network claim adjudication for out-of-network providers.

**Frequency:** 100% reproducible on any provider with mixed-status PNA records.

as per Description

## Solution

Open DR_GetActivePNAs DataRaptor Turbo Extract in OmniStudio DataRaptor editor. Add WHERE clause filter on the Provider_Network_Agreement__c object node: Status__c = 'Active'. Additionally add filter PNA__c.Effective_Date__c <= TODAY to exclude future-dated active PNAs from the primary panel (future-dated PNAs handled separately per INS-0097 logic). Validate fix in FS1 against 5 test provider records covering: (1) only active PNAs, (2) only terminated PNAs, (3) mixed active+terminated, (4) zero PNAs, (5) pending+active mix. Re-run full Provider 360 UAT regression suite. Review all other DataRaptors in the Provider360Card and Facility360Card for similar missing filter issues before next deployment. Document DataRaptor deployment checklist item to validate SOQL filters post-merge.
