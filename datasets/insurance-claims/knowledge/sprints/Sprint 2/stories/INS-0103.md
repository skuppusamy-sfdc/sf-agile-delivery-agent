## Description

As a Provider Network Specialist, I need a Provider 360 view on the Facility__c (Business Account) record that summarizes the Group Service Agreement (GSA), active network participation lines of business, and the affiliated individual provider roster so that I can manage facility contracts and ensure all rendering providers are credentialed and in-network.

This story is the facility counterpart to INS-0102 (Person Account providers).

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a Facility__c Business Account record is open,
When the Provider Network Specialist views the Provider 360 Facility page,
Then a GSA Summary panel displays: Group_Service_Agreement__c.GSA_Number__c, Group_Service_Agreement__c.Effective_Date__c, Group_Service_Agreement__c.Expiration_Date__c, Group_Service_Agreement__c.Status__c, Group_Service_Agreement__c.Contracted_LOBs__c (multi-select picklist rendered as tags).

Given the facility has an active GSA,
When the Network Participation panel renders,
Then each LOB listed in Group_Service_Agreement__c.Contracted_LOBs__c is shown as a row with its corresponding Fee_Schedule__c.Tier__c and Fee_Schedule__c.Rate_Effective_Date__c.

Given the facility has affiliated Provider__c Person Account records linked via the Facility_Affiliation__c junction object,
When the Affiliated Providers panel loads,
Then the roster displays: Provider__c.Name, Provider__c.Specialty__c, Provider__c.NPI_Verified__c (icon), Provider__c.Credentialing_Status__c, and a hyperlink to the individual provider's record.

Given any affiliated provider has Credentialing_Case__c records in status 'Pending Initial Review' or 'Information Requested',
When the affiliated providers roster renders,
Then that provider row is highlighted in amber and a tooltip reads 'Credentialing action required'.

Given the GSA expiration date is within 90 days of today,
When the GSA Summary panel renders,
Then a warning badge 'Contract Expiring Soon' appears next to Group_Service_Agreement__c.Expiration_Date__c.

## Solution

Build OmniStudio FlexCard 'Provider360_Facility_FC' on the Business Account (Facility__c) Lightning Record Page. DataRaptors: DR_Get_GSA_Summary (SOQL on Group_Service_Agreement__c WHERE AccountId = :recordId AND Status__c = 'Active'), DR_Get_Network_LOBs (parse Contracted_LOBs__c multi-select and join to Fee_Schedule__c), DR_Get_Affiliated_Providers (query Facility_Affiliation__c junction and join Provider__c fields including Credentialing_Case__c subquery for open cases). GSA expiration warning implemented via a DataRaptor Transform formula field comparing TODAY() to Expiration_Date__c. Amber row highlight driven by a FlexCard conditional style class. Deploy alongside INS-0102 in the same OmniStudio package version.
