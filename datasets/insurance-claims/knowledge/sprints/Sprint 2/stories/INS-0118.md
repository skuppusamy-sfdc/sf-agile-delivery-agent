## Description

As a Claims Operations Manager, I need a COB (Coordination of Benefits) indicator panel on the Policyholder 360 page so that I can quickly identify policyholders who carry coverage with multiple insurers and ensure claims are adjudicated with the correct primary/secondary payer sequencing.

This panel must reflect data captured during FNOL and updated through the claims lifecycle.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a policyholder has more than one Policy__c record where Policy__c.Status__c = 'Active',
When the COB panel renders on the Policyholder 360 page,
Then a 'COB Active' badge must display and list each policy with Policy__c.Carrier_Name__c, Policy__c.Group_Number__c, Policy__c.COB_Order__c (Primary/Secondary/Tertiary), and Policy__c.Effective_Date__c.

Given a Claim__c record is associated with a policyholder flagged for COB,
When the Claims Operations Manager views the open claim in the 360 panel,
Then Claim__c.COB_Flag__c must display as 'True' and Claim__c.Primary_Payer__c must be populated.

Given Policy__c.COB_Order__c = 'Primary' for one policy and 'Secondary' for another,
When the COB panel is rendered,
Then policies must be sorted ascending by COB_Order__c with the primary payer listed first.

-Given the panel includes a 'Request COB Verification' button that opens a DocuSign envelope, When clicked, Then a pre-filled COB attestation form is sent to the policyholder's email on file- (deprecated — DocuSign COB integration deferred to Phase 2)

Given no secondary coverage exists,
When the COB panel renders,
Then a 'No COB on File' label must display and the panel must not show an empty table.

## Solution

Extend the Policyholder_360_FC OmniStudio FlexCard (INS-0116) with a new 'COB_Status' card state. DataRaptor Extract DR_Extract_COBPolicies queries Policy__c filtered by AccountId and Status__c = 'Active' with a count check. A separate DataRaptor Transform aggregates COB_Order__c values. Conditional FlexCard state logic shows 'COB Active' vs 'No COB on File' based on record count. COB badge styling uses FlexCard custom CSS classes. Claim__c.COB_Flag__c is a formula field checking parent Policy__c COB data, computed via Apex formula helper. Permission-restricted visibility via FlexCard conditions tied to 'Claims_Ops_Read' Permission Set.
