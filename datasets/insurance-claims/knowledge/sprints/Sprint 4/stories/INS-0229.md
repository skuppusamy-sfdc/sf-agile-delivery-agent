## Description

As a Claims Operations Manager, I need a guided FNOL intake OmniScript that supports both Auto and Health LOB claim submissions so that claims are captured accurately and consistently at first notice, reducing downstream rework and manual data entry errors.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a logged-in Claims Operations rep opens the FNOL OmniScript,
When they select LOB = 'Auto' or LOB = 'Health',
Then the script dynamically branches to LOB-specific question sets and creates a Claim__c record with Claim__c.Status__c = 'FNOL Received' and Claim__c.LOB__c populated accordingly.

Given the rep enters a Policy Number on the intake screen,
When the policy lookup executes,
Then the OmniScript DataRaptor retrieves the matching Policy__c record and auto-populates Claim__c.Policy__c, Claim__c.Policyholder_Name__c, and Claim__c.Effective_Date__c.

Given all required FNOL fields are completed,
When the rep clicks 'Submit FNOL',
Then a Platform Event (FNOL_Submitted__e) is published and a Claim__c record is created with a system-generated Claim__c.Claim_Number__c in format 'CLM-{YYYY}-{000000}'.

Given the submitted claim has Claim__c.LOB__c = 'Health',
When the FNOL record is saved,
Then Claim__c.Provider__c lookup is required and must resolve to an active Provider__c (Person Account) with Provider__c.NPI__c populated.

-Given the rep uploads attachments during FNOL, When more than 3 attachments are added, Then a warning modal is displayed- (deprecated — no attachment limit enforced in Phase 1).

Given the FNOL OmniScript is submitted successfully,
When the confirmation screen renders,
Then the Claim Number, Date of Loss, and assigned Claims queue are displayed to the rep.

## Solution

Build a multi-step OmniScript (Type: FNOL_Intake, Sub-Type: Claims) with conditional branching driven by LOB__c picklist. Use DataRaptor Extract to perform real-time Policy__c lookup by Policy_Number__c. On final step, invoke an Integration Procedure that (1) creates the Claim__c record via DataRaptor Transform/Load, (2) publishes FNOL_Submitted__e Platform Event consumed by a Record-Triggered Flow to assign the claim to the appropriate Claims queue via Assignment Rules. Auto-number Claim__c.Claim_Number__c using a custom Apex auto-number utility class. Deploy OmniScript as an LWC embedded on the Claims App Home page.
