## Description

As a policyholder accessing the Agent Portal, I need a guided self-service OmniScript to submit a First Notice of Loss (FNOL) so that I can initiate a claim without calling the claims center, reducing inbound call volume and accelerating claim intake. Extends INS-0446 (Policyholder 360 profile page).

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a policyholder is authenticated on the Agent Portal,
When they navigate to 'File a Claim',
Then an OmniScript 'FNOL_SelfService_OS' launches and pre-populates Policy__c.Policy_Number__c, Policy__c.Effective_Date__c, and Policy__c.Expiration_Date__c based on the logged-in user's associated Policy__c record.

Given the OmniScript is on Step 1 (Incident Details),
When the policyholder enters a Date of Loss prior to Policy__c.Effective_Date__c or after Policy__c.Expiration_Date__c,
Then an inline validation error displays: 'Date of Loss must fall within your active policy period' and the Next button is disabled.

Given the policyholder completes all required steps,
When they click 'Submit Claim',
Then a Claim__c record is created with Claim__c.Status__c = 'Draft', Claim__c.FNOL_Submitted__c = true, Claim__c.FNOL_Channel__c = 'Self-Service Portal', and Claim__c.Date_of_Loss__c set to the entered value.

Given the Claim__c record is created,
When the OmniScript DataRaptor Transform executes,
Then a confirmation number derived from Claim__c.Name is displayed on the final OmniScript step and emailed to the policyholder via a Salesforce Email Alert.

Given the policyholder uploads supporting documents in the OmniScript,
When up to 5 files are attached (PDF, JPG, PNG; max 10 MB each),
Then ContentDocument records are linked to the new Claim__c via ContentDocumentLink with ShareType = 'V'.

Given the FNOL submission is complete,
When a Claims Operations Manager reviews the queue,
Then the new Claim__c appears in the 'FNOL Intake' list view filtered by Claim__c.FNOL_Submitted__c = true AND Claim__c.Status__c = 'Draft'.

## Solution

Create OmniScript 'FNOL_SelfService_OS' (Type: FNOL, SubType: SelfService) with 6 steps: Policy Lookup, Incident Details, Involved Parties, Document Upload, Review & Submit, Confirmation. Use DataRaptor Extract to pre-populate policy data. Use DataRaptor Transform + Load for Claim__c creation. Implement a Remote Action step calling an Apex class 'FNOLDocumentUploadController' to handle multi-file ContentVersion creation and linking. A Record-Triggered Flow on Claim__c (After Insert, when FNOL_Submitted__c = true) fires an Email Alert to the policyholder and publishes a Platform Event 'FNOL_Submitted__e' consumed by the Claims Operations assignment queue.
