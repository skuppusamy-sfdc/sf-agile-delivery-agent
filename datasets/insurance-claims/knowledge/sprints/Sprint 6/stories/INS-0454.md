## Description

As a Broker Relations Coordinator, I need a guided multi-step OmniScript on the Agent Portal that allows brokers to submit a First Notice of Loss (FNOL) on behalf of a policyholder, so that claims intake is accelerated and manual data re-entry by Claims Operations staff is eliminated.

Extends INS-0441 (Internal FNOL OmniScript) — broker-facing variant with restricted field set and community user context.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given a broker user with an active Policy__c record (Policy__c.Status__c = 'Active') associated to their broker code,
When the broker initiates 'File a Claim' from the portal,
Then the OmniScript Step 1 pre-populates Policy__c.Policy_Number__c, Policy__c.Policyholder_Name__c, and Policy__c.Effective_Date__c from a DataRaptor Load using the selected Policy__c Id.

Given Step 2 (Loss Details),
When the broker submits date of loss, loss description, and LOB-specific loss type picklist,
Then Claim__c.Date_of_Loss__c, Claim__c.Loss_Description__c, and Claim__c.Loss_Type__c are validated: Date_of_Loss__c must not be a future date and must fall within Policy__c.Effective_Date__c and Policy__c.Expiration_Date__c.

Given Step 3 (Claimant Info),
When the broker enters claimant details,
Then the OmniScript performs a duplicate-check DataRaptor against existing Contact records on TIN or SSN (last 4 masked) before creating a new Contact.

Given successful OmniScript completion,
When the broker clicks 'Submit Claim',
Then a Claim__c record is created with Claim__c.Status__c = 'FNOL Received', Claim__c.Source__c = 'Broker Portal', and Claim__c.Assigned_Broker__c stamped with the submitting broker's Contact Id.

Given the Claim__c record is created,
When the record is saved,
Then a Platform Event (FNOL_Submitted__e) is published triggering a Record-Triggered Flow that enqueues an assignment task for the Claims Operations Manager queue within 15 minutes.

Given the broker completes submission,
When the confirmation step renders,
Then the broker sees the system-generated Claim__c.Claim_Number__c and an estimated acknowledgment timeframe of '2 business days'.

-Given Step 4 (Document Upload), broker must upload at least one supporting document before submission- (removed per UAT feedback; documents now optional at FNOL stage)

## Solution

OmniScript ('BrokerFNOL_OS') with 6 steps: Policy Lookup, Loss Details, Claimant Info, Coverage Summary (read-only), Document Upload (optional, integrated with Salesforce Files), and Confirmation. DataRaptor Turbo Extract for policy pre-population. DataRaptor Load for Claim__c creation with field mapping. Integration Procedure orchestrates duplicate contact check and Platform Event publish (FNOL_Submitted__e). Record-Triggered Flow on Claim__c (after insert, Status__c = 'FNOL Received') creates a Case and assigns to Claims_Ops_Queue. Community user sharing handled via Guest Profile Sharing Rule on Policy__c scoped to Broker_Code__c.
